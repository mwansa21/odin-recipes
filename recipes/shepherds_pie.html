Shepherd's Pie

Description

Shepherd's Pie is a savory meat pie topped with mashed potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2-3 medium carrots, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 1/2 lbs ground beef
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 1 cup frozen corn
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 cups mashed potatoes (prepared in advance)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add the chopped onion and carrots, and cook until they begin to soften, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute.
  5. Add the ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon.
  6. Stir in the beef broth, tomato paste, Worcestershire sauce, and thyme. Let it simmer for about 5 minutes.
  7. Add the frozen peas and corn. Cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Spread the beef mixture evenly in a baking dish.
  10. Spread the mashed potatoes on top of the beef mixture.
  11. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, until the top is golden and the filling is bubbly.
  12. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
