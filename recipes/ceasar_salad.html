Caesar Salad

Description

A classic Caesar salad with crunchy croutons and a creamy dressing.

Ingredients

  • 1 large head of romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 1/2 cup Caesar salad dressing

Steps

  1. For the dressing, combine mayonnaise, garlic, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, Worcestershire sauce, grated Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper in a bowl. Mix well until smooth.
  2. In a large salad bowl, combine the chopped romaine lettuce, grated Parmesan cheese, and croutons.
  3. Drizzle the Caesar dressing over the salad and toss to combine.
  4. Serve immediately.
