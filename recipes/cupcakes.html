Cupcakes

Description

This is a simple and delicious cupcake recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix the dry ingredients together.
  3. Mix the wet ingredients together.
  4. Combine the wet and dry ingredients.
  5. Pour the batter into cupcake liners.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes.
